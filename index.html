Focused on listening

Soundbar plays your Soundcloud music without being needy all the time, hidden in your menu bar.

Use shortcuts to skip a song or to save it for later. Fully customizable.

Notifications

Always know what Soundbar is playing at all times. No need to open Soundbar. It’s especially handy when you use custom keyboard shortcuts.
